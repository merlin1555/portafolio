About us?

Get to know BioPlast: Innovators in Biodegradable Plastics.

Inspired by generations of experience, we have taken a turn toward sustainability with our line of biodegradable products.

Our legacy drives us to lead the change toward a cleaner, more responsible future. Each of our products not only represents quality and durability, but also an unwavering commitment to the environment.

Join us on this journey towards a world where plastic and sustainability go hand in hand. At BioPlas, we are transforming the industry, one biodegradable packaging at a time.

Our mission

At BioPlast, our mission is to lead the transformation towards a sustainable future by producing and supplying biodegradable plastic solutions. We are dedicated to innovating in the field of eco-friendly materials, offering products that combine quality, functionality and a genuine commitment to environmental protection. Our goal is to satisfy the needs of our clients and consumers, providing sustainable alternatives that significantly contribute to reducing the environmental footprint, promoting positive change in the industry and in consumer practices globally.

Our vision

La visión de BioPlast es ser reconocida como líder y referente en la industria de los plásticos biodegradables, impulsando la innovación y estableciendo nuevos estándares de sostenibilidad en el mercado. Aspiramos a inspirar y catalizar la transición hacia prácticas más verdes en sectores industriales y entre los consumidores, promoviendo un ciclo de vida del producto que sea plenamente circular y respetuoso con nuestro planeta. Nos comprometemos a ser una fuerza motriz en la creación de un mundo donde los materiales sostenibles sean la norma, no la excepción, contribuyendo así al bienestar de las futuras generaciones y al mantenimiento de la biodiversidad terrestre.

Why Choose BioPlast?

At BioPlast, we believe that the future of our planet is in our hands. We aspire to be catalysts of positive change towards a more sustainable world, for this reason, we are dedicated to innovating in the plastics industry, offering products that not only meet the needs of our customers but also take care of our Earth. Choosing BioPlast means joining a movement that values ​​the well-being of our environment as much as business success.

How do we do it?

At BioPlast, we transform sustainability into action through our commitment to innovation and excellence. Our processes are designed to minimize environmental impact, using biodegradable materials and responsible production practices. We strive to be at the forefront of technology, ensuring that each product is not only eco-friendly but also of the highest quality. By choosing us, you choose a company that puts caring for the planet and innovation at the center of everything it does.

What we offer?

BioPlast is your trusted partner in biodegradable packaging solutions, offering a wide range of products designed to fit your needs without compromising the environment. Each item in our catalog is a testament to our commitment to quality and sustainability. By collaborating with us, you benefit not only from exceptional products, but also from the opportunity to be part of an environmentally friendly solution. We invite you to work with us and be part of a sustainable future!

Waste management:

Chile is taking giant steps towards sustainability and environmental responsibility. The adoption of PLA takes on special relevance in this context, offering an ecological and sustainable alternative to conventional plastics. Its use in a variety of applications, from food packaging to disposable products, represents a significant change in how we think about and use materials in our daily lives, aligning us with a greener and more sustainable vision.

Automation and Workplace Wellbeing:

Automation improves efficiency and safety, creating an optimal work environment for our team, without work overload.

Energy efficiency:

Starting in September, we will use state-of-the-art machinery to maximize energy efficiency, allowing us to significantly reduce our carbon footprint during the production process.

Controlled Production:

Our production model works based on demand to avoid overproduction and unnecessary waste, thus ensuring a greener and more efficient operation.

Customization for Large Demands:

We offer customized solutions for companies that need large quantities of specific packaging, adapting to their needs without compromising our ecological values.

Support for the Local Economy:

As national producers, contribute to the strengthening of Chile's economy, supporting local industry and reducing the need for imports.

Reduction of Costs and Emissions in Shipping:

By choosing BioPlast, you will avoid additional costs and unnecessary emissions associated with international transportation, favoring a more sustainable and economical option.
